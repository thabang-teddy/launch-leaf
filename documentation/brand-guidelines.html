04 — Clear space

Always maintain clear space equal to the diameter of the orange circle around the full logo mark. Never crowd it with other elements, text, or imagery.

06 — Do's and don'ts

Do

  • ✓Use the logo on approved backgrounds only
  • ✓Keep "Launch" in navy and "Leaf" in orange in the wordmark
  • ✓Scale the mark proportionally at all times
  • ✓Maintain minimum clear space around the mark
  • ✓Use approved lockup variants (horizontal / stacked)

Don't

  • ✗Recolour either element of the mark
  • ✗Stretch, rotate, or skew the logo
  • ✗Place on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
  • ✗Add shadows, outlines, or decorative effects
  • ✗Use below 28px — use the icon-only mark at small sizes
